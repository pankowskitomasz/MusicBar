FAQ

What is a theme party? A theme party is a party that focuses on a specific theme or subject and all of the decorations are centered around that.

What are some popular themes? Some popular themes include "80's Night", "Retro Disco Night", and "Superheroes Party".

What should I wear to a theme party? It's always best to wear something that fits the theme of the party. For example, if it's an 80's Night, wear an 80's outfit.
